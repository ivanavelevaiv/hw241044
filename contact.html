Number1: Turkey

Turkey has always had a special place in my heart. I’ve been visiting every summer since I was 7 years old, and it truly feels like my second home. The beautiful beaches, the warm weather, the amazing food, and the friendly people make it my ultimate childhood go-to place. Every year brings new memories, but the feeling of walking along the seaside or exploring the colorful bazaars always stays the same. I simply love it there!

  • About the country

    Turkey is a beautiful country located at the crossroads of Europe and Asia. It is known for its rich history, stunning beaches, and delicious food. From the lively streets of Istanbul to the relaxing coastlines of Antalya, there is always something to explore.

  • Must Visit

    People visit Turkey for many reasons. It has beautiful beaches, rich history, and amazing food. Tourists love exploring ancient cities like Istanbul, relaxing at seaside resorts, and trying traditional Turkish dishes like kebabs and baklava.

Fun facts

    Turkey is the only country that lies on both Europe and Asia.

  • The Home of Troy

    The famous ancient city of Troy, known from Greek mythology, is located in Turkey.
